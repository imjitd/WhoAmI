Who Am I

I'm JIT (Soumyojit). I was born on 5th October 2006 in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. I am currently 19 years old.

I completed my Higher Secondary education from Habra High School with Mathematics, Computer Application, Accounts, and Cost & Tax.

Curiosity

I consider myself a person who is passionately curious.

From the age of six or seven, I used to open almost every electronic item I could get my hands on — from solar-powered lights to remote control cars. Not to break them, but to understand them.

I kept a small toolbox filled with electronic scraps and components. With those pieces, I constantly tried to create something — even if most of the time it didn’t work. I didn’t know the theory. I simply enjoyed experimenting.

Beginning of Programming

At the age of 15, I started learning programming using a second-hand Samsung Galaxy J7 Next and free YouTube videos.

I did not have advanced resources. But I had something more important — the same curiosity I had as a child: the desire to understand how things work and how they can be improved.

What I Am Exploring

Today, I explore the intersection of technology, business, and problem-solving.

I am especially interested in building things that are useful and solve real problems — even in small ways.

This website is a space where I document what I learn, what I build, what I question, and what I am trying to become.

My Belief

I believe curiosity is more powerful than talent, and steady effort is more important than speed.

If you are learning, experimenting, and trying to build something meaningful — you are welcome here.
